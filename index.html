Elite Bridge Features

A comprehensive platform for caregiver staffing and facility management.

👤 Staff Features

📋 Shift Browsing

Browse available shifts at multiple facilities with detailed information about location, timing, and compensation.

💰 Earnings Tracking

Track your monthly earnings, view payment history, and manage your financial information securely.

⏱️ Clock In/Out

Easily clock in and out for your shifts with real-time tracking and automatic time calculations.

🔄 Shift Swapping

Request shift swaps with other caregivers or pick up available shifts in the marketplace.

⭐ Ratings & Reviews

Build your professional reputation with ratings from facilities and other caregivers.

📱 Mobile First

Fully responsive design optimized for iOS and Android devices for on-the-go access.

👨‍💼 Admin Features

👥 Staff Management

Manage staff profiles, certifications, background checks, and performance metrics.

📅 Shift Creation

Create and manage shifts with flexible scheduling and automatic staff matching.

📊 Analytics

View comprehensive analytics on staff utilization, application conversion, and facility performance.

💳 Payment Processing

Process payments securely with Stripe integration and generate tax documentation.

🔔 Notifications

Send SMS and email notifications for shifts, reminders, and important updates.

🎯 Smart Matching

Intelligent algorithm matches staff to shifts based on availability, skills, and ratings.

🔒 Security & Compliance

Background Checks

Integrated Checkr API for automated background verification and compliance.

Data Encryption

End-to-end encryption for sensitive data including payment and personal information.

Compliance

HIPAA-compliant platform for healthcare staffing with audit trails and reporting.

🛠️ Technology Stack

Frontend: React Native, Expo, TypeScript, NativeWind (Tailwind CSS)
Backend: Node.js, Express, tRPC, Drizzle ORM
Database: MySQL with TiDB Cloud
Integrations: Checkr (Background Checks), Stripe (Payments), Twilio (SMS), SendGrid (Email)
Deployment: Expo Go, EAS Build, GitHub Pages
